Admissions

Welcome to the admissions page of St Paul High School. We are delighted that you are considering our school for your child's education. Here, we provide you with all the necessary information and guidelines to make the admissions process as smooth as possible.

Why Choose St Paul High School

At St Paul High School, we strive to provide an exceptional educational experience that nurtures the growth and development of every student. Here are some reasons why you should choose our school:

  • Highly qualified and dedicated teachers
  • A diverse and inclusive learning environment
  • State-of-the-art facilities and resources
  • A comprehensive curriculum that promotes critical thinking and creativity
  • Opportunities for extracurricular activities and personal growth
  • A strong focus on character development and values
  • Parent involvement and open communication

Admissions Process

To apply for admission to Standrad College Ntungamo, please follow the steps outlined below:

  1. Complete the online application form on our website or obtain a physical copy from the school office.
  2. Submit the required documents, including:
    • Birth certificate of the student
    • Previous academic records (if applicable)
    • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
    • Passport-sized photographs
    • Proof of address
    • Medical records and immunization history
  3. Once the application is submitted, our admissions team will review the information and contact you to schedule an entrance test and/or interview.
  4. After the evaluation process, you will be notified of the admission decision.
  5. If your child is accepted, you will receive further instructions regarding enrollment and payment of fees.
